Procurement

Procurement.

Emtwenty Co. specializes in procuring hardware, software, and licensing tailored to meet your requirements at the best prices. Emtwenty Co. offers local stock management, custom provisioning of spare parts, hardware support, and worldwide storage and logistics. Whether it's servers, desktops, laptops, peripherals, consumables, networking equipment, or cloud services like Microsoft Azure and Amazon Web Service, Emtwenty Co. is here to assist!

Our team of certified technicians is available to provide timely assistance and resolve any hardware-related issues that may arise, minimizing disruption to your business operations. Furthermore, we offer worldwide storage and logistics solutions to meet the needs of businesses with global operations. Whether you're expanding into new markets or managing distributed teams, our logistics capabilities ensure that your hardware and software products are delivered securely and efficiently wherever they are needed. At Emtwenty Co., we are committed to providing comprehensive procurement solutions that help you optimize your IT investments and achieve your business objectives. Let us take the hassle out of sourcing and managing hardware, software, and licensing, so you can focus on what you do best—growing your business. We understand the importance of SaaS licensing in today's digital landscape. As part of our procurement services, we assist clients in identifying and selecting the right SaaS solutions to meet their business needs. Whether it's productivity tools like Microsoft 365 or collaboration platforms like Slack, we help clients navigate the complex landscape of SaaS offerings and ensure that they choose solutions that align with their requirements and budget. Emtwenty Co. also recognizes the importance of BYOL in optimizing software licensing costs and simplifying cloud migration efforts. We assist clients in identifying opportunities for BYOL and optimizing their license usage in cloud environments. Whether it's deploying virtual machines on Microsoft Azure or Amazon Web Services, we help clients leverage their existing licenses to minimize costs and streamline operations.
